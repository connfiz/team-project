McHugh's Coffee Pod

About us


McHughs coffee pod is a family owned business based in western Ireland whose goal is to provide coffee lovers a portable coffee vending service which would be able to visit different venues and festivals which would be in need of our service. McHughs initially began In 1952 when Peter McHugh saw an opening at animals shops and fairs and chose to set up a voyaging container selling hot cups of tea and newly made sandwiches to the ranchers who assembled around. Beginning around 1985 Peter and his significant other Frances McHugh have been running McHughs and have acquired a priceless standing all over the area serving quite hot cups of free tea and very new specially made sandwiches to ranchers, dealers and passers by hoping to fuel up for the afternoon.




Our shop

In our shop we will be selling numerous coffee beans to our customers and providing a homemade cooking book to help you with your coffee creating fantasies.
